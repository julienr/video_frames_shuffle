Video frame shuffling experiment

This is an experiment inspired by Temporal Integration Windows for Natural Visual Sequences. In this experiment, researchers shuffled the frames of a video in a random order and evaluated the difficulty to follow the video after shuffling. Shuffling is done over window of a given size.
